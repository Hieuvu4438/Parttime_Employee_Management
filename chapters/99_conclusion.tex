\chapter*{Conclusion}
In conclusion, the proposed Part-time Employee Management System provides a structured framework for managing shift-based part-time employees within a restaurant chain environment. The system encompasses key functional modules, including weekly shift registration, scheduling management, payroll calculation, and performance statistics.
\\\\
By incorporating priority-based scheduling, attendance-based wage computation, overtime handling, and penalty calculation, the system enhances fairness, operational efficiency, and payroll accuracy. The analysis and design presented in this project demonstrate the practical application of software engineering principles to address real-world organizational requirements.
\\\\
Although further improvements and technical refinements may be considered in future development stages, the project successfully fulfills its primary objective of designing a comprehensive and logically structured workforce management solution.
